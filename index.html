Netiquette & Email Security

5 key points — Mobile-ready

This short guide covers essential ideas for polite online communication and secure emailing.

Five Main Points

1. Respect & Tone

Treat others politely online—tone can be misread.

  • Use greetings.
  • Avoid ALL CAPS.
  • Pause before replying angrily.

2. Clarity & Purpose

State your purpose clearly.

  • Use clear subject lines.
  • Short paragraphs.

3. Privacy & Consent

Respect personal data.

  • Ask before sharing.
  • Use BCC for groups.

4. Account Security

Protect your login details.

  • Strong passwords.
  • Enable 2FA.

5. Phishing & Links

Fake links are common online.

  • Check URLs.
  • Don’t open unknown files.
